About

Mission Driven Purpose (MDP) is a non profit organization based in New York. MDP provides outreach, training, consulting, products and motivational speaking in order to mobilize people.

Mission Statement:

To spread the knowledge of a loving God to uplift humanity. We are driven by the love of Christ and a pasion to see humanity uplifted. We accomplish this in various ways.

What We Do

MDP Training Program:

MDP sees the value of young people therefore it trains Christain youth groups in various churches on personal spiritual and effective witnessing. In addition we train on community awareness, comminuty involvement, career choices budgeting, leadership, and similar practical life skills. Our training also consists of practicality . Our field exercise include personal witnessing, literature evangelism, hospital programs and youth led evangelistic meetings.

MDP Awareness Program:

MDP also exists to raise community awareness on health and wellness. This includes health expositions, clean-up exercises and cause walks.

MDP Mission Trips:

MDP has a special focus on international effors in Africa. It sponsors volunteer driven missions to alleviate poverty, hunger, basic necessities, health education, and spiritual comfort. With such a mission and strong drive, our ultimate purpose is the glory of God!
